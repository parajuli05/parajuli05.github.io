My Design & Development Process

A structured, user-centered approach that turns ideas into high-performing digital products from discovery to launch and beyond.

01

Research & Discovery

Understanding users, business goals, competitors, and constraints through interviews, analytics, and market research.

02

User Flows & Wireframing

Mapping user journeys, information architecture, and low-fidelity wireframes to validate structure early.

03

Visual Design & Prototyping

High-fidelity UI design, motion design, micro-interactions, and interactive prototypes in Figma / Framer.

04

Usability Testing & Iteration

Testing with real users, gathering qualitative & quantitative feedback, refining designs based on insights.

05

Development & Handoff

Clean, responsive code implementation, component libraries, performance optimization, and smooth developer handoff.
